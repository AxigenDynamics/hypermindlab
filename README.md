# hypermindlab

Hypermind Lab

Hypermind Lab is an applied research laboratory, product development and experimental systems focused on the intersection of artificial intelligence, decision science, and operational systems.

The laboratory develops experimental technologies, research frameworks, and prototype systems designed to improve human decision-making and AI-assisted analysis.

Research activities within Hypermind Lab include:

retrieval-augmented knowledge systems
decision science and structured analytic methods
AI transparency and governance
reproducible research infrastructure
applied operational analytics

This repository serves as the public research index for Hypermind Lab.

Operational systems and production code are maintained in separate repositories, while this repository documents experimental systems, research frameworks, and methodology.
